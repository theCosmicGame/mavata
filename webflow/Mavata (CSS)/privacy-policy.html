Privacy Policy

Updated January 4, 2022.
‍
Your privacy, and the privacy of the information provided to Mavata, is important to us. This Mavata Privacy Policy (“Privacy Policy”) explains how we use, disclose, transmit, store and share protect personal information.

A reference to “Mavata,” “we,” “our,” or “us” is a reference to Mavata LLC.
‍

Mavata as a Service Provider

Mavata customers are private businesses who use our services for market intelligence, cost optimization, and M&A preparation and initiation. Our customers may provide personal data which we may process to provide our services. If you have questions about your personal information entered on a Mavata service by one of our customers, or you want to exercise any of your rights regarding your personal data, our customer agreement requires that we redirect your inquiry to the applicable Mavata customer.
‍

Personal Information We Collect

From You‍
‍Mavata collects personal information directly from you when you interact with us online (including via the Mavata website), in person, or via other means of communication.

If you communicate with us, you may choose to provide us with your personal data, including name, company name, email address, phone number, street address, and any other information you choose to provide us. If you choose to be a customer of Mavata, you may provide to us credit or debit card data or other payment information.

We may collect and retain a record of our communications with you.

Please do not provide us with personal information that you do not want us to have.

From Technology
Mavata may use technology (including cookies, web beacons, and other similar technologies) to monitor your activities, interactions, preferences, IP address and other connection information relating to your use of our website and services.  We may also use similar technologies in our emails to you to collect information, including whether you opened the email or engaged with the email or its contents.

We may also use third-party web and email analytics services to monitor your use of our website and our communications. These services help us to observe how visitors use our website and our communications.
‍
From Third Parties
Mavata may collect information about you from other sources, including from public websites, social media, professional or personal networks, public documents, press releases and third parties from whom we have purchased business contact information. Business contact information may include your name, company name, your title, email address and phone number. In some instances, we may combine personal information you have provided to us with information that we receive from third parties. We process this information to update or expand our marketing records, identify new customers, expand our network, improve our service offering, and enhance our marketing.

How We Use Personal Information

Mavata may aggregate the information we collect for any purpose, including research, marketing, and commercial activities.

We may also use your information to:

  • Provide our services;
  • Communicate with you, including responding to your inquiries submitted via our website;
  • Send marketing emails that we believe may be of interest to you;
  • Personalize our correspondence with you and your experience on our website;
  • Improve our marketing and website;
  • Enhance our service offering;
  • Recruit talent to join our organization;
  • Perform internal research and analysis;
  • Detect or mitigate inappropriate, malicious, fraudulent or illegal activity;
  • Comply with laws; and
  • Manage and troubleshoot issues with our website.

How We Share Personal Information

We do not sell personal information that we collect about you, but we may sell or share anonymous or aggregated data based on the personal information in our possession. Any aggregated information will not personally identify you. We do not disclose the information that we obtain about you, except as described in this Privacy Policy.

We may disclose and/or transfer your information in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent;
  • To our third-party service providers or vendors for a legitimate business purpose;
  • As permitted or required by law or regulation;
  • To comply with a valid administrative or judicial order;
  • To any person or entity under a reasonable duty of confidentiality to Mavata that has agreed to keep such personal information confidential;
  • To complete, endeavor to complete, or enter into negotiations related to a reorganization, merger, sale, acquisition, stock offering, bankruptcy, or other corporate transaction.

Information Retention

Mavata will retain your personal information for at least as long as necessary to fulfill the legitimate business purpose for which it was collected or as required pursuant to law or regulation.
